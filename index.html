Our Story

a little bit about EELAB

EELAB AGRO is a climate-smart Agri-marketplace. We are continuously enhancing our understanding and creating new modern and commercially viable ways to enhance and implement carbon sequestration into the Agri ecosystem. We work on enhancing Agri productivity, increasing competence to fight shocks at all levels, and creating ways to increase the possibility of carbon sequestration wherever possible. We provide more and more sustainable and proprietary ways to implement the process which are modern and also increasing revenue streams for all stakeholders. By doing this, the one who provides us with what we need, can provide us with what we need and prosper. Thereby creating an ecosystem that is profitable as well as beneficial for all.

About Us

Vision

EELAB® is conceptualized to develop sustainable climate-smart Agri chain Models supporting rural, marginal farmers and vendors through a targeted and professional approach and gain economic viability as a company.

Mission

To achieve a carbon sequestrated production and strengthen the supply chain for Agri input and output and, in turn, make farming more organized and professional for Indian farmers.

  • Enhancing agricultural productivity sustainably
  • Reduce and forecast shocks at all levels of the chain
  • Reducing greenhouse gas emissions and increasing carbon sequestration where possible
  • Make every person in the chain from farmers to customers climate warriors
